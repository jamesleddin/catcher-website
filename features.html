Your home screen

See exactly what you're owed, the moment you open the app.

Catcher's home screen surfaces your total potential savings across every finding it has uncovered — from forgotten subscriptions to insurance overpricing. No dashboards to configure. No graphs to decode.

Categorised spending across the top, claims-in-progress tracked at the bottom. One screen, full picture.

What companies owe you

Every overcharge in one place, with the law on your side.

The Savings tab groups every finding by type — duplicate charges, subscriptions to review, price increases, insurance hikes — with the exact figure Catcher can recover for you.

Built-in citations to the right regulator mean every claim is grounded in real consumer protection law.

Subscription inventory

Every recurring charge, tracked forever.

Catcher confirms every subscription on your bank and shows the real monthly and yearly cost — so you stop "guessing" what you spend on Netflix, Spotify, ChatGPT, your gym and the rest combined.

Tap any one to review or cancel. Even the ones you dismissed earlier stay on the list — nothing slips back into the background.

One tap to fight back

Catcher drafts the letter. You hit send.

For every finding, Catcher prepares a complete claim — the merchant, the amount, the relevant law, the regulator. Duplicate charges become refund requests. Subscriptions become cancellations. Insurance hikes become renegotiations.

Question-led actions mean Catcher never assumes — you confirm, it acts.

Claims, tracked

Watch every claim move from "sent" to "settled."

The Claims tab tracks every letter Catcher sent on your behalf. Ready to Claim, In Progress, Resolved — with day counters and follow-up reminders so nothing dies in a provider's inbox.

If a provider goes silent past their statutory window, Catcher escalates automatically.

Smart Money

Understand your spending without the lectures.

Smart Money shows where your money went this month with a clean category donut and a side-by-side comparison to your typical pattern. Coaching, not policing — anomalies surface automatically.

Tap any category to drill into the merchants behind it. Every euro accounted for, no transaction-scrolling required.

Goals you'll actually reach

Save with a specific destination in mind.

Set a goal — a trip, a deposit, a new car — and Catcher tracks every saving you find against it. Verified refunds, cancellations and renegotiations auto-credit toward your goal so you can watch it grow week to week.

Ask Catcher for a personalised plan: flight prices, daily budget, booking tips, all sourced for your destination and date.

What Catcher Catches
A full catalogue of overcharges

Every finding type Catcher detects today — with the action it drives in your account.

🚗
Highest Value

Insurance Renewal Overpricing

Car, home, health, pet, travel and life insurance renewals often come in higher than new-customer quotes. Catcher spots the jump and drafts a negotiation letter with competitor rates.

"Your car insurance renewal came in at €843. Three competitors quoted €560–620. Here's your negotiation letter."
📺
Most Common

Subscription Creep

Streaming services, gym memberships, apps you forgot about. Catcher identifies recurring subscriptions and gives you one-tap cancellation instructions with the provider's contact pre-filled.

"You're paying €35.97/month across 3 streaming services. Want a side-by-side comparison?"
💸

Duplicate Charges

Card-based duplicate charges from restaurants, retail, and online purchases happen more than you'd think. Catcher spots them and drafts your refund request.

"You were charged €47.80 twice by the same merchant on March 14th. Here's your refund letter."
📈

Price Increases

When a provider raises your price without proper notice, Catcher catches it and drafts a complaint citing the relevant regulator — energy, telecoms, financial services, or general consumer rights.

"Your mobile bill went up €5/month. Here's your complaint draft to the telecoms regulator."
🏷️
Day 1 Finding

Hidden Installment Interest

Paying insurance in monthly installments typically adds 15–25% in hidden interest. Catcher spots this and shows you cheaper financing alternatives.

"Your monthly insurance payments include an estimated €180/year in hidden interest. A 3% loan could save you €140."
⏰

Free Trial Traps

That "free trial" just became a paid subscription. Catcher catches the first charge and gives you instant cancellation steps before the next billing cycle.

"A streaming service just charged you €9.99 — your free trial ended yesterday. Cancel now?"
✈️
EU261

Flight Delay Compensation

Catcher scans your bank for airline purchases, checks if your flight was delayed 3+ hours, and drafts an EU261 claim letter. Up to €600 per passenger — no claims company taking a large cut.

"Your flight on 12 March was delayed 4hrs. You may be owed €250 per passenger under EU261. Here's your claim letter."
📋
End-to-End

Claim Tracking & Escalation

Catcher doesn't just draft the letter — it tracks every claim from send to resolution. Follow-up letters at day 7, counter-offer responses, and escalation drafts to the right regulator (FSPO, CRU, ComReg, CCPC in Ireland — more on the roadmap) if companies don't respond.

"Day 7 of 10. No response yet. Catcher drafted a follow-up letter."
🏠
New

Rent & Recurring Transfers

Most apps lump P2P rent transfers in with random payments to friends. Catcher spots recurring same-recipient debits with stable amounts and a monthly cadence — and asks if it's rent. One tap and it's tracked as Housing across every spending screen.

"You send €1,313 to John Smith every month — is this rent? Yes → counted as Housing forever."
🔔

Needs-Input Notifications

When Catcher finds transactions that need your judgement — possible rent, savings transfers, low-confidence findings — it bundles them into a single weekly nudge. No spam, no per-finding pings, no notification fatigue.

"3 transactions to confirm — tap to review. One notification, not three."
