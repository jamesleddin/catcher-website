FAQ
Questions you should ask
Is my bank data safe? ▾
Catcher connects via TrueLayer, a regulated Account Information Service Provider authorised by the Central Bank of Ireland. We only have read-only access — we can never move money. We never see or store your login credentials. All data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM and stored in the EU.
Which Irish banks are supported? ▾
At launch: AIB, Bank of Ireland, Revolut, N26, and Permanent TSB. More banks and credit unions coming soon.
How do the AI letters work? ▾
When Catcher detects an overcharge, AI generates a professional letter citing specific Irish regulations — FSPO for insurance, CRU for energy, ComReg for telecoms. It includes real competitor quotes when available and pre-fills the provider's email. You review before sending. Always your name, always your control.
When do I actually pay? ▾
Only after your bank data confirms the charge dropped. Not when you send the letter. Not when the company responds. Only when real money is saved in your actual bank account. If Catcher doesn't save you money, you pay nothing.
Can I delete my data? ▾
Yes, at any time. One tap removes everything — your account, transactions, findings, and letters. We disconnect from your bank immediately and revoke all access tokens. GDPR Article 17 — your right to erasure.
What about spending targets? ▾
Catcher lets you set spending targets per category — linked to your savings goals. If you're saving for a holiday, Catcher shows you how staying under your dining target gets you there faster. Alerts at 80% and 100%, coaching tone, never guilt.
