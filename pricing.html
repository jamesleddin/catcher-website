Pricing FAQ
Pricing questions
How does pricing work? ▾
Catcher Free includes subscription detection, claim tracking, and one AI letter per month — free forever, no card required. Catcher Pro (€7.99/month or €69.99/year) unlocks unlimited letters across every overcharge type, all Smart Money features, AI-powered goal planning, and multi-account support.
Can I switch between Free and Pro? ▾
Yes — upgrade or downgrade at any time from your account settings. If you downgrade mid-cycle, Pro features remain active until the end of the billing period.
Is there a free trial of Pro? ▾
Catcher Free isn't a trial — it's a real product you can use forever. If you want to try Pro, the annual plan (€69.99) works out to €5.83/month and includes the equivalent of two free months over a year.
How is Catcher paid? ▾
Subscription only. Catcher does not take a percentage of money you recover, does not earn commission on switches, and does not sell your data. You pay for Catcher; Catcher works for you.
Can I cancel Pro anytime? ▾
Yes — one tap from your account settings, no questions asked. You'll keep Pro access until the end of the billing period and revert to Free afterward.
